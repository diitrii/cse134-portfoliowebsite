AI Agent Study Assistant

April 2025

This was the project that I made at DiamondHacks, where the end product was a study assistant that used agentic AI to keep users focused, and an Arduino kit to measure temperature levels and brightness of the surrounding area.
The first part of the project used an AI agent in order to check to make sure you were on task. If you switched tabs too often, a pop-up would come up and would tell you to stop switching tabs. If you were idle for too long, a pop-up would appear and tell you to get back on task. And finally, a pop-up would appear when you needed to take a break.
The second half was a temperature sensor, which helped us to gauge whether the temperature was too hot, too cold, or just in the right space. We had a breadboard with multiple lights, and the green meant everything was optimal, yellow too dark, red too hot, and blue too cold. We tested it by blowing on the sensor to heat it up, and by covering the sensor with our hands to remove light.
